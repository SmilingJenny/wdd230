Jennifer Walton's Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

My site name is: Provo Chamber of Commerce.

I chose this name because it is straight forward and follows normal naming conventions.

Site Purpose

The Provo Chamber of Commerce site is a rich hub of resources for local business owners. We seek to promote Connection, Cooperation, and Economic Prosperity through facilitating conversations and efforts with local government and community leaders.

Scenarios

  • What services do you offer members?
  • How can I become a member?
  • What is the contact information for the chamber's board?
  • What are the local policies that are being discussed or implemented, that will impact me or my business?
  • What news or events are there in the community that effect me?
  • What are the other businesses in the community?
  • Where can I find the latest economic report or projections for the city?

Color Schema

Blue: #205893 - H1/title, logo, major sections. May also be used for background color and links, socials, or for sub-headings to contrast with the orange.

Charcoal:#242325 - Paragraphs, link markers, and borders. May also be used for background color, icons, socials.

Orange: #D2691E - Section headings and accents. May also be used for buttons, socials, wayfinding, and call to action items.

Typography

EB Garamond

I wanted a more professional feel for the site name, logo, and titles of major sections, so I went with a serif typeface: EB Garamond

Montserrat

I wanted a lighter feel for the h2-h6 headings and paragraphs that was engaging and easy to read, so I went with a sans-serif typeface: Montserrat.
