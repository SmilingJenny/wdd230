Attractions

  • Brigham Young University (BYU): Brigham Young University is a private research university located in Provo. Visitors can explore its beautiful campus, attend sporting events, visit the Museum of Art, and enjoy performances at the Harris Fine Arts Center.
  • Provo Canyon: Provo Canyon offers stunning natural beauty and outdoor recreational opportunities. Visitors can hike scenic trails, go fishing or kayaking in the Provo River, enjoy a picnic in the mountains, or take a scenic drive along the Alpine Loop.
  • Bridal Veil Falls: Bridal Veil Falls is a picturesque waterfall located in Provo Canyon. Visitors can hike to the base of the falls, take in the stunning views, and enjoy a picnic in the nearby park. In the winter, the falls freeze over, creating a beautiful ice climbing destination.
  • Utah Lake State Park: Utah Lake State Park is a popular destination for boating, fishing, and picnicking. Visitors can rent kayaks, paddle-boards, or sailboats, go fishing for bass or catfish, or simply relax on the sandy beach.
  • Provo City Center Temple: The Provo City Center Temple is a historic building and a prominent landmark in downtown Provo. Visitors can take a guided tour of the temple grounds, admire its stunning architecture, and learn about the history of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the area.

A History Overview of Provo, Utah

Founded in 1849 by Mormon pioneers, Provo, Utah, quickly flourished as a center of agriculture, trade, and entrepreneurship.


The fertile Utah Valley soil supported thriving farming communities, while Provo's strategic location along the Old Spanish Trail facilitated vital trade routes.


Over time, Provo diversified its economy, becoming a hub for manufacturing and later embracing technology and innovation.


The establishment of Brigham Young Academy (now Brigham Young University) in 1875 further fueled the city's growth, fostering a culture of education and entrepreneurship that continues to thrive today.


With a legacy of resilience, innovation, and collaboration, Provo stands as a vibrant city with a dynamic business community, attracting entrepreneurs, startups, and established companies seeking opportunities for growth and success..
